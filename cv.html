Current Position

Assistant Professor, Department of Communication Sciences and Disorders and Department of Neurology

Research Interests

Auditory neuroscience and sensory processing. Perception and encoding of synthetic and complex sounds in the neocortex. The role of inhibition in sound perception and processing in auditory cortex. Speech and music perception. Auditory working memory.
